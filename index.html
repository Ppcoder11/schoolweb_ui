MODERN VIDYA NIKETAN ARAVALI HILLS
Best education Builds best future

‘The Future Of The Nation Is Getting Scripted In The Schools’.

A vibrant classroom gives way to a resurgent nation. Modern Vidya Niketan Aravali Hills was founded in 2000 by Sh. Gopal Sharma, an educationist, a pioneer & a visionary who aspired to provide insightful education with world-class facilities. Here, at MVN, Aravali Hills, modernity meets traditions, with a firm belief in character formation, ethos, moral values, and attention to spiritual, intellectual, and social development. A quest for learning and scientific temperament is intrinsically infused in every student. Sprawling across acres of lush green valley, the Aravali’s heart, the institution is hailed as an architectural marvel incorporating the latest infrastructure. MVN Aravali Hills has taken giant strides and carved a niche in the realm of academic excellence. The sterling performance of students with District toppers in CBSE Board Exams, competitive examinations be it IIT, NEET, KVPY, NTSE, etc. bears silent testimony to the formidable reputation that the school has earned. An extensive scholarship program named after founder Principal Shri. Gopal Sharma provides 100% scholarships to meritorious students. Exhaustive study material and assignments, comprehensive, all-encompassing examination system with OMR assessments and Online Test Series evaluate preparedness. Faculty for SAT facilitate admissions abroad, workshops, and career counseling sessions are held. Special educator caters to CWSN to ensure inclusive education. The school boasts of a unique distinction of being a pioneer in Faridabad in adopting VRV system, Ipad labs, laptop labs, language labs, a specially designed play area for experiential learning, all-weather swimming pool, skating rink, TT, Basketball and football grounds, gymnastics et al. Regular yoga and fitness classes spur the physical, mental and spiritual development of the students. MVN ensures proper safety measures through continuous CCTV surveillance; biometric attendance and GPS-enabled transport with a live tracking facility. Cultural exchange programs and visits to NASA, Hong Kong, and Europe have been smoothly amalgamated with the school curriculum. The school is proud to be associated with TERI. Instituting a Sewage Treatment Plant in the school, planting trees, and composting are steps towards contributing towards a greener tomorrow. The meteoric rise that MVN Aravali Hills has been witnessing is deftly scripted by ever-supportive Management, a battery of highly experienced and dedicated faculty motivated by a proactive Principal and cooperative parents' body.
